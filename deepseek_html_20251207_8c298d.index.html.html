About CMB

Global supplier specializing in children's art products, committed to providing safe and high-quality painting products for children worldwide

Our Mission: Let Every Child Create Safely

Shanghai CMB Import & Export Co., Ltd. was established in 2005, specializing in the research, development, production, and global supply chain management of children's art products. We understand that safety is paramount for children's products, so all our products strictly adhere to international safety standards, ensuring they are non-toxic, eco-friendly, and safe.

Our team consists of experienced product designers, quality inspection experts, and international trade specialists, familiar with children's product regulations in various countries. We collaborate with multiple internationally certified factories to ensure every step from raw materials to finished products meets the highest quality standards.

We have successfully exported products to over 50 countries and regions worldwide, establishing long-term partnerships with numerous well-known brands and retailers. Whether OEM customization or private label products, we can provide professional solutions.
